# Data dictionary

This folder mixes real market data with two clearly marked limits. Read this
file before you trust any number here.

## Official or real (fetched from the named sites)

- `tunindex_universe.csv`: every company listed on the Tunis Stock Exchange
  as shown on ilboursa.com on 13 July 2026. The `sector_guess` column is our
  own label, not an official classification. Treat it as a rough grouping,
  not a source-confirmed sector code.
- `daily_snapshot_2026-07-13.csv`: TUNINDEX level, Tunisian Bond Index,
  Brent crude price, and EUR/USD to TND rates, all read live from
  ilboursa.com on 13 July 2026.
- `daily_movers_2026-07-13.csv`: the top four gainers and top four losers
  of the day, with ticker, price, and percent change, read from ilboursa.com
  on 13 July 2026. One ticker (SMD) could not be matched with confidence to
  a company name in the alphabetical list, so its name field says
  "unconfirmed company match" rather than guessing.
- `tunisie_valeurs_model_portfolio_2025.csv`: the 11-stock core portfolio
  and 5-stock dividend-yield basket recommended by Tunisie Valeurs for
  2025, taken from the article "Stratégie d'investissement et portefeuille
  type : les recommandations de Tunisie Valeurs" on
  leconomistemaghrebin.com, published 2 January 2025.
- `tunisia_macro_indicators_2024.csv`: GDP growth, unemployment rate, and
  listed-company profit growth, cited in the same article and attributed
  there to INS (Institut National de la Statistique) and the IMF.

## Not retrievable

We could not pull usable content from bmcecapitaltunisie.com through our
fetch tool. The page loaded with no readable body text. No data from that
site appears in this repo. If a future run of this project needs BMCE
Capital's research, someone will need to open the site directly and copy
the relevant figures by hand.

## Generated by this repo, not fetched

- `portfolio_log.csv`: starts with one real row (the 13 July 2026 TUNINDEX
  close). Running `run_log_snapshot.py` on a later date adds a new row, so
  this file grows into a real time series the more you use the tool.

## A note on tickers

ilboursa.com's alphabetical company list gives full names, not ticker
codes. We only include a ticker where ilboursa's own movers list showed
one directly (BHASS, LNDOR, TINV, BTE, SMD, ASSAD, TRE, AST). We did not
invent ticker codes for the rest of the universe list, since a wrong
ticker on a real, named company is worse than no ticker at all.
